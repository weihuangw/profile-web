Dispersion

Audio-Visual Live Performance

2020


The work observes how atoms spread and dissolve in worlds of different dimensions. By constantly changing the viewing perspective, it looks at the many sides of the same element. From a single point to a solid object, everything is made of tiny atoms. In the visuals, points, lines, and surfaces come together and move through space in an organic way.


The work uses real-time, computer-generated sound and images. The visuals are paired with broken electronic beats, allowing viewers to explore a pure sensory experience through the close connection between sound and image.
